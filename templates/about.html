About Rockville

Building a smarter, more connected community through technology and civic engagement

Our Mission

The Rockville City Portal was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform Rockville into a more responsive, transparent, and community-driven city by bridging the gap between residents and city services.

Why This Matters

Every day, residents encounter issues in their community - potholes, broken streetlights, overflowing trash bins, damaged benches, and more. Too often, these problems go unreported or unresolved due to lack of an easy reporting system.

Our platform provides a simple, accessible way for residents to report issues, track their resolution, and stay informed about what's happening in their neighborhood. By making civic engagement effortless, we're building a stronger, more connected Rockville.

Community First

We believe that the best solutions come from the community itself. Every report, every suggestion, every interaction helps make Rockville better.

Key Features

  • GPS location mapping

Discover and create community events that bring neighbors together. From block parties to volunteer cleanups, build stronger connections in your neighborhood.

  • Event creation & discovery
  • RSVP functionality

Real-time Updates

Stay informed about the issues you care about. Get notifications when your reports are being processed, when work begins, and when problems are resolved.
